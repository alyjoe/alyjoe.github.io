The purpose of each page

  1. Home Page - This page welcomes my viewers and gives them the option to send an email to me if they so desire.

  2. My Resume and Career Information Page - This page allows my viewers to learn of my career objective, experience, education history and highlights, my additional skills, Vision statement and mission for my career, job requirements and qualifications needed for my career choice.

  3. My Personality Page - This page informs my viewers of my strongest personality trait, my hero and why I chose that person to be my hero.

  4. My Personal Development Page - This page informs my viewers of how I can apply what I have read from chapters 4-7 of "Twelve Pillars" to my life. Additionally, it displays a summary of the chapters that I read.

  5. Storyboard Page - This page shows my viewers the wireframe for each page in the website, displays a list of the pages in my website and their intended purpose, displays the theme of the site and displays a list of features I intend to use on my website.

  6. Reference Page - This page displays the bibliographic sources of information that I have used for assistance for this website.

The theme of the Website

The colour theme I used for this website is blue, white and pink because my website gives off an aura of success and calmness as well as the simplicity of it. When one views my website, they should feel calm and relaxed. The target audience of my website is any member of the public.
